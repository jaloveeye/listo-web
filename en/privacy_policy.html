2. Period of Processing and Retention of Personal Information

Listo processes and retains personal information within the period of retention and use of personal information in accordance with laws and regulations, or within the period of retention and use of personal information agreed upon when collecting personal information from the data subject.

3. Items of Personal Information Processed

Listo does not operate any servers, and all data is stored only on the user's device. No data is transmitted to servers or collected externally.

Listo stores the following information only on the user's device.

  • Optional Items
    • Academic calendar information (when calendar is linked)

Note: The minimum device information required for notification service provision is automatically processed by the operating system, and this information is not transmitted to servers and is used only within the device.
